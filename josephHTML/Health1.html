I'm Joseph Moiselle and I'll be writing about the impacts that hunger can have on health.


I will discuss the effects that hunger can have on childrens health specifically.

Children who go hungry for even a short period of time can have severe lifelong impacts.


Research has shown that when a mother has low iron levels, it can have an impact on their ability to comprehend language and follow directions during the first five years of the child's life. Iron deficiencies in newborns and toddlers has been observed to have massive developmental delays and hinders the childs ability to be successful in a classrom environment.

Research also shows that childhood hunger can lead to mental health issues such as depression in the persons adolescence and adulthood. It can also cause mood, substance abuse and behaviour disorders.

There are also physical health detriments caused by hunger, on the minor end of the scale, the child will have stunted growth but could also develop illnesses such as:

  • Type 2 diabetes
  • Anemia
  • Rickets
  • Coronary heart disease
  • Osteoperosis
  • Stroke
  • Cancer
  • & more
